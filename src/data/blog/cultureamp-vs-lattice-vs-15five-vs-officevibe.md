### What this means in practice

- Culture Amp is typically the most “enterprise‑oriented” from a pricing and packaging perspective: everything is quote‑based, annual, and often bundled across Engage, Perform, and Develop.  
- Lattice publishes clear per‑seat prices but also enforces a $4k annual minimum, which means very small teams may pay more per active user than list price implies. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- 15Five is the most transparent about engagement vs performance packaging, with a relatively low entry price for Engagement and a mid‑range price for the full platform, all on annual contracts. *Source: [15Five pricing page](https://www.15five.com/pricing/), [15Five pricing FAQ](https://www.15five.com/pricing-old2025/)*  
- Officevibe stands out for its straightforward $5/user/month pricing and 10‑user minimum, making it easy to budget for smaller teams-especially if you just need engagement and recognition without deep performance or comp features. *Source: [Workleap – Officevibe pricing](https://workleap.com/pricing/officevibe/)*  

Pricing can also vary significantly by region, employee count, contract length, and negotiated discounts. Always double-check current prices with each vendor's calculator or sales team.

## What are the key features of each platform?

### Culture amp

*Core positioning: Culture Amp markets itself as a “market‑leading employee experience platform” built on people science, analytics, and a massive benchmark dataset.*  

**Key Features:**

- Research‑backed engagement platform with 40+ ready‑to‑use survey templates, including engagement, DEI, onboarding, exit, and more.  
- Science‑backed pulse surveys plus AI‑generated comment summaries to quickly surface themes and recommended actions.  
- Extensive benchmarks powered by over 1 billion survey responses across industries and geographies, giving strong context for scores.  
- AI Coach that turns survey data into tailored action plans and communication guidance for managers, embedded directly in the Engage workflow.  
- Integrated Perform and Develop modules for goals/OKRs, performance reviews, 1:1s, and career paths, plus platform‑wide people analytics.  

**Best For:**

- Organizations that want survey science, benchmarks, and analytics to drive a sophisticated employee listening strategy.  
- Global companies needing multi‑language support and strong data privacy/compliance (GDPR, SOC2, ISO 27001). *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
- HR teams that plan to unify engagement, performance, and development under one vendor with a strong services layer (People Scientists). *Source: [Culture Amp G2 description](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  

### Lattice

*Core positioning: Lattice calls itself “the HR platform people love,” combining performance, engagement, goals, compensation, and AI into a single people success system.* *Source: [Lattice homepage](https://lattice.com/), [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*  

**Key Features:**

- Core performance suite including structured performance reviews, talent reviews, promotions, PIPs, calibrations, and AI‑assisted review writing. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- Goals & OKRs with cascading goals, Salesforce and Jira integrations, reminder notifications, and customizable views. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- Engagement add‑on with pulse surveys, eNPS, onboarding/exit surveys, and AI‑powered survey insights and recommended actions. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- Manager Tools for 1:1s, feedback & praise, weekly updates, and Q&A boards, tightly integrated with performance and goals. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- Lattice AI Agent and analytics to answer employee questions, surface insights, and save HR time by automating manual workflows. *Source: [Lattice homepage](https://lattice.com/)*  

**Best For:**

- Mid‑market to larger organizations that want a single platform for performance, engagement, and compensation with strong analytics. *Source: [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*  
- Companies where performance and goal management are the primary drivers, with engagement as an integrated add‑on. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
- HR teams looking to lean into AI for HR help desk, writing assistance, and performance summaries. *Source: [Lattice homepage](https://lattice.com/)*

## What are the strengths and weaknesses of each platform?

### Culture amp

**Strengths:**

- High overall satisfaction (4.5/5 from 1,516 G2 reviews), indicating strong product‑market fit across engagement and performance use cases. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
- Users repeatedly praise intuitive reporting and dashboards, noting it’s “easy to gather and analyze employee feedback in one place” with clear trend insights. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
- People‑science focus and large benchmark dataset are differentiators: Culture Amp emphasizes research‑backed surveys and over 1 billion benchmark responses.  
- Strong implementation and support, with reviews calling out helpful People Scientists and office hours as key to successful rollout. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  

**Weaknesses:**

- G2’s aggregated pros/cons list “missing features,” “limited features,” and “limited customization” among top cons, especially around reporting and filtering. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
- Some admins report frustrations with report configuration (e.g., generating manager‑specific engagement reports can require workarounds). *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
- Several reviewers mention alert fatigue and time‑consuming review cadences for managers, especially in large organizations. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  

### Lattice

**Strengths:**

- Highest rating in this group (4.7/5) with over 4,000 reviews, suggesting strong and consistent satisfaction, particularly among mid‑market companies. *Source: [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*

**Weaknesses:**

### 15Five

## Which platform should you choose?

### Choose culture amp if:

1. **You care most about survey science and benchmarks.** You want research‑backed questions, 40+ templates, and industry‑leading benchmarks derived from over 1 billion responses to drive your engagement strategy.  
2. **You’re a global or multi‑region organization.** You need broad language support, strong compliance (GDPR, SOC2, ISO), and enterprise‑grade security baked into your engagement stack. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
3. **You want engagement, performance, and development fully integrated.** You plan to run engagement surveys, performance reviews, 1:1s, and career paths in one place and value People Scientist support to guide program design. *Source: [Culture Amp – Employee engagement platform](https://www.cultureamp.com/platform/employee-engagement), [G2 – Culture Amp](https://www.g2.com/products/culture-amp/reviews)*  
4. **You have budget and appetite for a quote‑based enterprise tool.** You’re comfortable negotiating annual PEPM contracts and potentially paying more than SMB‑focused tools in exchange for depth and services. *Source: [Culture Amp pricing page](https://www.cultureamp.com/request-quote), [Capterra – Culture Amp Pricing](https://www.capterra.com/p/154170/Employee-Effectiveness/pricing/)*  
5. **You want AI‑assisted action planning inside engagement.** AI Coach and AI comment summaries are important for turning feedback into prioritized actions and manager‑ready narratives. *Source: [Culture Amp – Employee engagement platform](https://www.cultureamp.com/platform/employee-engagement)*  

### Choose lattice if:

1. **Performance management is your primary driver.** You need robust performance reviews, goals/OKRs, and talent reviews-with engagement as an integrated but secondary layer. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing), [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*  
2. **You want a unified platform for performance, engagement, and compensation.** You plan to connect pay, performance, and engagement data in one place and value compensation modules alongside surveys. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  
3. **You’re mid‑market and growing.** Your company is roughly 50–1,500 employees, and you want a system “built to scale with your company” without immediately jumping to heavy enterprise HRIS suites. *Source: [Lattice vs Culture Amp compare page](https://lattice.com/compare/lattice-vs-culture-amp), [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*  
4. **You care about ease of use for managers.** You want highly rated UI, strong 1:1 and feedback tools, and high adoption from non‑HR users. *Source: [G2 – Culture Amp vs Lattice comparison](https://www.g2.com/compare/culture-amp-vs-lattice-lattice), [G2 – Lattice](https://www.g2.com/products/lattice-lattice/reviews)*  
5. **You’re okay with a $4k annual minimum.** Your team is large enough that the minimum contract doesn’t distort per‑user costs. *Source: [Lattice pricing page](https://lattice.com/pricing)*  

### Choose 15Five if:

1. **You want to operationalize weekly check‑ins and 1:1s.** Your main goal is to create consistent, structured manager‑employee conversations that keep teams aligned and engaged. *Source: [15Five Core Platform overview](https://success.15five.com/hc/en-us/articles/21354702192155-Core-Platform-Overview)*  
2. **You need integrated performance + engagement at SMB‑friendly pricing.** You’re attracted to clear Engage ($4), Perform ($11), and Total Platform ($16) tiers billed annually. *Source: [15Five pricing page](https://www.15five.com/pricing/), [15Five pricing FAQ](https://www.15five.com/pricing-old2025/)*  
3. **You care about manager effectiveness and coaching.** You see big value in Kona AI, manager training microlearnings, and a Manager Effectiveness Indicator baked into the platform. *Source: [15Five platform overview](https://www.15five.com/products/)*  
4. **Your org is small to mid‑sized and not extremely complex.** You don’t need heavy org modeling or extremely advanced analytics, and you can accept some admin complexity in exchange for strong end‑user experience. *Source: [G2 – 15Five](https://www.g2.com/products/15five/reviews), [PerformYard – Lattice vs 15Five](https://www.performyard.com/articles/lattice-vs-15five-vs-performyard)*
